## E-Commerce Sales & Profit Analysis

### Objective
Analyze e-commerce sales data to identify profit drivers, loss-making products,
and the impact of discounting on business performance.

### Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Plotly

### Key Insights
- Technology category contributes highest sales and profit
- High discounts lead to negative profitability
- Q4 months generate maximum revenue

### Business Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategies
- Focus on high-margin products
